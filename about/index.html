About


Music Blocks is an open source collection of manipulative tools for exploring fundamental musical concepts in an integrative and fun way.

Music Blocks is a fork of Turtle Blocks and was created by Walter Bender.

It is part of Sugar Labs, an organisation involved in developing learning platforms for children

Music Blocks is currently in V3.0
